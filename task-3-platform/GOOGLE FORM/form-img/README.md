PROJECT-REPORT

For my project, I used Google Forms as the main platform to create a Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz. The objective of this project was to spread awareness about digital literacy and test basic knowledge about online safety, responsible internet usage, digital footprints, cyberbullying, and identifying fake information online. Google Forms is a very useful tool for creating surveys, quizzes, and feedback forms, and this project helped me understand its features in a practical way.
While creating the quiz, I learned how to add different types of questions such as multiple-choice questions, short answer questions, and checkbox questions. I also learned how to make questions compulsory, divide the quiz into sections, and add descriptions and instructions for users. I explored features like answer keys, automatic grading, and response validation. Another important feature I used was linking Google Forms to Google Sheets so that responses could be collected and analyzed in an organized manner. This helped me understand how data collection and basic data analysis work.
Through this project, I practised digital content creation, survey design, question framing, and data organization. I also learned how to share the form through a link and collect responses from classmates or participants. This project improved my understanding of how online tools can be used for educational purposes and awareness campaigns. I also developed skills like logical thinking while framing questions, basic data handling, and digital communication.
These tools will help me academically in many ways in the future. I can use Google Forms to conduct surveys for research projects, create quizzes for group studies, collect feedback for presentations, and organize information efficiently. It will also help me in college projects where data collection from multiple people is required. Understanding digital tools like Google Forms is very important in modern education because many assignments, tests, and surveys are conducted online.
Overall, this project helped me improve my digital literacy, technical skills, and understanding of online tools used in education. It also made me more confident in creating digital content and using technology for academic and professional purposes. I believe the skills I learned from this project will be very useful for my future academic work, research projects, presentations, and collaborative learning activities.
