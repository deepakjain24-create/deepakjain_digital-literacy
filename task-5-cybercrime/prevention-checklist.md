**Stay Safe Online – Checklist (For College Students in India)**
**###🛡️ Stay Safe Online Checklist**

Follow these practical steps to protect yourself online:

1) Use strong and unique passwords for email, banking, and social media accounts. Avoid using your name or birthdate.
2) Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on Gmail, Instagram, banking apps, and important accounts.
3) Never share OTP, passwords, or UPI PIN with anyone — banks never ask for this information.
4) Always verify UPI payment requests before entering your PIN. Remember: You enter UPI PIN only to send money, not receive money.
5) Do not click suspicious links received through SMS, email, or WhatsApp, especially messages about prizes, refunds, or KYC updates.
6) Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or payments, as hackers can steal data from unsecured networks.
7) Log out from shared computers in college labs or cyber cafés after using email or social media.
8) Keep your phone and apps updated to avoid security vulnerabilities.
9) Download apps only from official stores like Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
10) Immediately report cyber fraud if money is lost or an account is hacked.


🚨 Reporting Cyber Crime in India

If you face online fraud, report immediately:

National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930

Reporting quickly increases the chances of recovering money.
